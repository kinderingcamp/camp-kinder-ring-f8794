Frequently Asked Questions About Family Camp Weekends

How much does a family camp weekend at Camp Kinder Ring in Patterson, NY cost, and what is included in the price?
Our family camp weekends are priced per family and typically range from $500 to $750, depending on the size of your group and the type of accommodations you choose (cabin or lodge). The price includes all meals, lodging, and access to our camp activities such as swimming, boating, arts & crafts, and sports. Additional activities like the ropes course or archery may require a small extra fee. Please contact us for current pricing and availability.
When do family camp weekends take place, and how far in advance should we book?
Family camp weekends are usually offered on select weekends from late May through early September. Because we’re located in Patterson, NY, these weekends tend to fill up quickly, especially around holidays. We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance to ensure your preferred date and accommodations. You can check available dates and make reservations directly through our website or by calling our office.
Is Camp Kinder Ring licensed and are staff on-site during family weekends?
Yes, Camp Kinder Ring is a fully licensed children’s camp with the State of New York and adheres to all local health, safety, and fire regulations. During family camp weekends, trained staff are on-site to supervise activities, manage facilities, and provide medical assistance if needed. Our waterfront is staffed by certified lifeguards during swimming and boating hours.
What is the process for checking in and what should we bring for a family camp weekend?
Check-in for family camp weekends typically begins at 4:00 PM on Friday, with check-out by 2:00 PM on Sunday. Upon arrival, our staff will greet you at the main lodge, provide a camp map, and review your weekend schedule. We provide bedding in our upgraded lodges, but if you’re staying in a cabin, please bring your own sheets or sleeping bags, towels, and toiletries. A suggested packing list is available on our website, and we’re happy to answer any specific questions you may have.
What happens if it rains or we need to cancel our reservation? Do you offer any guarantees or refunds?
We offer a variety of indoor activities and covered spaces to ensure your family still has a great experience, rain or shine. If you need to cancel, please let us know as soon as possible. For most weekends, cancellations made more than 30 days in advance receive a full refund minus a $50 administrative fee. Cancellations within 30 days are subject to a partial refund, depending on the circumstances and our ability to rebook your space. Please review our full cancellation policy on our website or call our office for details.
