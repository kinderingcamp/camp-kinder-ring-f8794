Frequently Asked Questions About Birthday Party Camp Packages

What is included in Camp Kinder Ring's birthday party camp package, and how much does it cost?
Our birthday party camp packages include exclusive access to our outdoor and indoor camp facilities in Poughkeepsie, a dedicated party host, organized activities (like archery, crafts, and boating in season), use of picnic areas, and all necessary party supplies. Pricing starts at $475 for a three-hour party for up to 20 children, with options to add catering, cake, or additional activities for an extra fee. We provide a detailed breakdown of inclusions and add-ons during the booking process.
How far in advance should I book a birthday party at Camp Kinder Ring, especially during peak season?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for parties held between May and September, as those dates fill up quickly. Some weekends are reserved for regular camp activities, so early booking ensures your preferred date and lets us customize your party to your needs.
Is Camp Kinder Ring licensed and insured to host children's birthday parties?
Yes, Camp Kinder Ring is fully licensed by New York State as a children's camp and carries comprehensive liability insurance for all hosted events. All of our staff are background-checked and trained in child safety, and we have certified lifeguards present for any water activities.
What is the process for planning a party, and can we visit the camp beforehand?
After you submit an inquiry, our party coordinator will reach out to discuss your needs, confirm available dates, and walk you through activity options. We encourage families to schedule a complimentary tour of our Poughkeepsie campgrounds before booking, so you can see the spaces and ask questions in person. Once your date is reserved, we’ll handle all planning and send you a detailed itinerary.
What happens if it rains or there's bad weather on the day of our party? Do you offer any guarantees?
We have indoor spaces and covered pavilions available, so parties can continue rain or shine. If severe weather makes it unsafe to hold your event, we’ll work with you to reschedule at no extra charge or provide a partial refund if rescheduling isn’t possible. Your child’s safety and enjoyment are our priorities.
