Frequently Asked Questions About Summer Day Camp Programs

What are the session dates and daily hours for Camp Kinder Ring's summer programs in Chelsea Cove?
Camp Kinder Ring’s summer day camp programs in Chelsea Cove typically run from late June through mid-August, divided into two- and four-week sessions. The camp day begins at 9:00 AM and ends at 4:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We also offer early drop-off starting at 8:00 AM and late pick-up until 5:30 PM for families who need extended care. Specific session dates for each summer are available on our website or by contacting our office.
How much does it cost to enroll my child at Camp Kinder Ring, and are there any sibling discounts?
Tuition at Camp Kinder Ring for the 2024 season is $2,100 for a two-week session and $3,900 for a full four-week session. We offer a 10% discount for each additional sibling enrolled. The price includes all daily activities, lunch, snacks, and transportation within a 10-mile radius of Chelsea Cove. A $300 deposit per child is required to secure a spot, with the balance due by May 1st.
Is Camp Kinder Ring licensed and are your staff trained in safety and first aid?
Absolutely. Camp Kinder Ring is fully licensed by the New York State Department of Health and undergoes annual inspections to ensure compliance with all safety and health regulations. All staff members are background-checked, and our counselors are trained in CPR and First Aid. We also have a certified nurse on-site at all times to handle any medical needs that may arise.
What is the enrollment process, and how do I secure a spot for my child?
To enroll your child at Camp Kinder Ring, start by completing our online registration form or visiting our Chelsea Cove office. Once you submit the form and pay the $300 deposit, your child’s spot is reserved. We’ll follow up with a welcome packet, a medical form to be completed by your child’s physician, and additional information about transportation and daily schedules. Orientation for new families is held in early June.
What happens if my child is unhappy or needs to withdraw from camp—do you offer refunds or guarantees?
We strive to ensure every camper has a positive experience. If your child is unhappy, our counselors and camp director will work closely with you to address concerns. If you need to withdraw before June 1st, your tuition (minus the deposit) is fully refundable. After June 1st, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis for medical or family emergencies. We do not offer guarantees, but our priority is to create a supportive, enjoyable environment for every child.
